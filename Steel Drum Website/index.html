Welcome to your new Digital Steel Drum!!

Made by Helena, Josh and Emmet

Here is our main project if you want the source code and here is our documentation!

This is our steel drum and you can toggle between flat, sharp and major here.
The controls are:
SPACE or R-CLICK to toggle between modes.
ARROW KEYS or your steel drum Makey Makey to play the sounds.

We hope you enjoy the fun little project!
