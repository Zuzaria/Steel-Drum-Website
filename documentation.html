Process

Research

We figured that to even do this correctly, we needed to know how a steel drum worked, so we looked at some references to help us out.

In doing so, we also learned how certain notes would work and in a way to make it easier for us and the user, we designed it do that you may switch between different variants of notes via one tap of a button!

Design

Our Drafts

This is one of the drafts Emmet came up with for the steel drum and we went off of these. I declared that there should be different tones, so we would need three modes which was perfect for us since we all needed to contribute art of some kind; allowing us to make some kind of artistic flare for each mode.
